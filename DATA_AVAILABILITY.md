# Data Availability

The raw liquid-level monitoring records and original sensor coordinates cannot be publicly redistributed because they contain restricted municipal infrastructure information controlled by the data owner. Public release of these records could reveal the location and operating status of drainage assets. The data owner approved public release of transformed demonstration data and derived figure/table source data.

The public GitHub repository is available at https://github.com/huangyuzhou-ops/urban-drainage-data-quality-response, and the versioned archive is available from Zenodo at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20422917. The public archive provides figure- and table-level source data, environment and configuration files, an anonymization utility and a small anonymized demonstration monitoring dataset with relative time indices. These materials support inspection and numerical checking of the reported figures and tables. They do not include the restricted raw monitoring records, original asset identifiers, original coordinates, or the complete restricted-data workspace required for full reruns. Access to the raw operational records is controlled by the data-owning organisation and is subject to its infrastructure-security and project-governance review.
